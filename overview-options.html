Overview redesign — four directions

Four ways to make the Overview clearer. The core fix in all of them: make the scope of every number obvious, give the page one clear focal point, and make the empty current-season state read as intentional rather than broken.

Season toggles are illustrative only. Pick the one that clicks, or mix and match, and I will build it into the live dashboard.

Option 1

Gameday Hub

Who we play next, and whether we are ready.

Clarity comes from a single dominant focal point: one large NEXT MATCH hero owns the screen with opponent, countdown, logistics, and a plain-language head-to-head verdict, plus one obvious next action. Every number is now explicitly scoped with a label ("Next fixture", "Recent form", "Last result"), so nothing reads as a broken zero. The empty current-season stats are moved entirely off the Overview behind a "Full table" link, and the never-won counter is demoted to a slim footnote ribbon, so the page answers one question well instead of mixing all-time and current-season data.
